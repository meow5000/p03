About Meﾟ✧

Motion Graphic Designer & Video Editor

Hi! My name is Daphne and I am a passionate motion graphics designer and video editor. My journey in this field began at a young age when I started experimenting with video editing on my first iPod Touch. Over the years, my love for storytelling grew as I discovered the power of motion graphic typography music videos and it’s impactful storytelling through dynamic animations. I believe that the stories a piece communicates will leave a lasting impression on audiences, and video and motion design are able to do that effectively. At Simon Fraser University, I have honed my skills and knowledge in editing films in a film course and transforming graphics into animations in a media course. My goal is to continue to use motion to my graphic design work to enhance storytelling and connect with audiences on a deeper level.
