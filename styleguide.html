Ethos:

Biography:

Cinnabox is an online shop that sells collectable blind box figures of the Sanrio character, Cinnamoroll and his friends. There are many different series to chose from. Test your luck to get your favorite character or collect the whole family!

Project Analysis 1

While Cinnabox's collectibles appeals for enthusiasts of all ages, the primary target demographic spans from children to young adults. The diverse range of figures are curated to resonate with the whimsical and imaginative interests of younger individuals, while young adults appreciate the nostalgic charm and artistry.

Project Analysis 2

There are many ways that Cinnabox entices someone to purchase a product. Firstly, blind boxes offer an element of surprise and excitement. The suspense and anticipation of not knowing what you will get keeps customers engaged. Furthermore, because of their collectable aspect, customers will be motivated to continue purchasing these figures. With a wide range of collections, there is something for everyone.
